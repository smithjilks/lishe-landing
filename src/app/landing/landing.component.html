About

Everyday food is wasted yet so many people go hungry. In Kenya alone food loss and wastage stands at Ksh 72 Billion annually. UNEP and Waste Resources Action Program estimates that every Kenyan throws away 99kg (281 pounds) every year yet one in every three Kenyans is found poor.
Globally the wastage stands at $940 billion which is estimated to be a third of the global food production. Lishe tries to solve this problem by allowing users with excess food to share it with the food insecure. This can be done at an individual level, through charity organisations or through businesses such as restaurants and supermarkets.
Lishe does not aggregate demand for food for producers(or processors) and farmers. It is not an e-commerce platform.
